Celestials' Hackathon 2022

Celestials’ Hackathon 2022 brings together a clan of developers, designers and IT students, all joining hands for a 24 hour long program that is with the theme of ‘Smart Education’ with the intent to build groundbreaking solutions to the Education problem recurring here in Nepal.

deadline: 24 Aug, 2022

who are organizing?

we are priviliged to annouce that IT club of Shree Shanti Model Secondary School, Celestials have gotten to organize this program with the utmost pride.

who can participate?

anyone who has knowledge and interest in coding, +2 level students, Bachelor level Students and enthusiasts.

more about event.

The program embarks at sharp 5:00 pm in the evening and ends at sharp 5:00 pm of the next day. It is going to be a 24 hours long program where the internet connectivity, power supply and food services are availed by the organizers of the program.

  • teams can register representing their college or form team of individuals.
  • each team can have 2 to 4 members in it.
  • venue of this program is Shree Shanti Model Secondary School.
  • the regisration fee is Rs 2,000 per team.
  • participants should register the team before the deadline.
